Our Story

BUILT ON PASSION,
INKED WITH PRIDE

SIBI Tattoo Studio opened its doors in Tirupur in 2025, founded by SIBI with one simple goal .— to give every client a tattoo they'll be proud of for life, not just another design off a wall.

What started as a one-man passion for the craft has grown into a studio trusted across Tirupur for clean line work, original artwork, and a space where clients feel comfortable from consultation to the final wipe-down.
